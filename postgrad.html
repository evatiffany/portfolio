Here are some notable projects I have completed during my Master candidature (2020-2021) in the M.Sc in Urban Science, Policy and Planning (MUSPP) offered by the Humanities, Arts and Social Sciences (HASS) faculty in the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD).

Web-based Interactive Data Visualisation

Assignments and group project completed as part of 02.526 Interactive Data Visualisation course in Term 2 (January to April 2021). Visualisations were mostly created using the D3.js and Chart.js library. Hover over each item to access the project.

  1. Real Time Carpark Availability Table (data pulled from data.gov API)

  2. Interactive Bar Chart on number of victims aged 21 and under of major crimes in Singapore between years 2011-2019)

  3. Choropleth map of SG Male population by subzones in year 2020

  4. Choropleth map of total SG population by subzones in year 2020 (using Leaflet.js and OneMAP API for basemap)

  5. Total Government Expenditure by Ministry and Sector in SG Budget FY2021

  6. Group Project: Sentiment Analysis on TraceTogether news articles using Facebook reactions
